I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), where I have the privilege of being advised by Professor Minh Do. Prior to joining UIUC, I earned my Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University, where I was part of the IIIS Yao Class.

I am broadly interested in developing scalable, trustworthy machine learning models for complex real-world applications such as medical imaging and satellite remote sensing. My research spans both theoretical and empirical perspectives, with a particular interest in understanding the interplay between theory and methodology in machine learning.
